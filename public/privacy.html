Living Glass Aquarium — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Living Glass Aquarium collects nothing.

The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. It makes no network requests — every fish, plant, and sound is generated on your device. Your saved tanks and settings are stored only on your device and never leave it.

The same is true of the web version at aquarium.duski.org and the Apple TV app.
