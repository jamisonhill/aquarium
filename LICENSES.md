# Licenses & Credits

## Site content

**Every visual and audio asset in this project is generated procedurally at
runtime** — fish geometry and skin textures (canvas painting), plants, corals,
rocks, driftwood, substrate, backgrounds, particles, and all ambient audio
(Web Audio synthesis). No third-party models, textures, HDRIs, photos or
sound recordings are included or downloaded. There is nothing to attribute
and no redistribution risk.
